How To Make Ham & Potato Soup

Description

Sheet pan Italian sausage, roasted sweet potatoes, and sweet bell peppers are tossed with pasta,
plenty of parmesan cheese, and a sprinkle of arugula. Yes, please!

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Sausage
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Parmesan
  • Onion
  • Chili Flakes
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Roast Sausage & Veggies Break up and cook the sausage and the sweet potatoes on a sheet pan
  2. Cook Pasta Cook the pasta while the veggies are roasting.
  3. Toss & Enjoy: Toss the cooked pasta and roasted/sausage and veggies in a bowl with pasta water, parmesan cheese, lemon zest, and arugula.
