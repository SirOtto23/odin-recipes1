How To Make Crock Pot Mac & Cheese

Description

Homemade mac and cheese is my personal favorite food and I can assure you that this is the
best crock pot macaroni and cheese I have ever had!

Ingredients

  • Precooked Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Boil pasta for about 2 minutes less than the package indicates.
  2. Shred Cheese:While pasta is boiling, shred the sharp cheddar cheese.
  3. Mix: Put all of the ingredients into the slow cooker and cook
