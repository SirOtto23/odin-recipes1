How To Make Lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna may have a few steps, but each step is easy
and I assure you it is worth the time; the perfect Italian meal! The is a really easy lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic.
    Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
